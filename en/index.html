Purpose

Hi. My name is Terry Martin (aka @tzmartin). I’m the founder of Semantic Press, a developer of mobile apps, cloud solutions and training services.

As part of my daily routine, I am often researching on a wide range of topics related to mobile and cloud engineering. As I find them, I document various notes in my private journal or in a knowledge database.

Rather than keep this data hidden, I'd like to share the love. So think of it as tasty snippets for your brain to feast on regarding startups, technology and design.

I believe in people, ideas and the sharing of knowledge. I hope this site is useful to you.
